Our Story

When mentioning Aishaman, we want people to know us as one of the best travel agencies that exist in Malaysia. It is an initiative of 4 young people namely Aiman, Shafiq, Amni, and Fauzan where the dream of these four close friends is to build one agency that related to travelling and tourism. People are so crazy about traveling nowadays, so it makes this Aishaman travel agencies very popular and well-known.

We all know the tourism sector in our country is increasing post-Covid-19. This is because Malaysians prefer to travel within the country only. This makes the rate of tourism in the country even higher.

The states we have chosen as our recommended travel destinations are Kuala Lumpur, Selangor, Penang, Kedah, Perak, and Melaka. These 6 states were selected as preferred destinations because they have unique holiday attractions and can attract the attention of many tourists. Coincidentally, the four of us were also actually born in such countries.
